Who are we?

Frostlight Studios is an independent animation collective spread across 12 countries in 4 continents. We strive to rise from a humble beginning to deliver content people will be invested in in the present and nostalgic over in the future! From our beginning as a small path branching off from the Knights of Ardonia studio, we have evolved into an open and collaborative environment driven by our dedication and ambition!
